Design Process for All

WORKSHOP

We are in the middle of cultural change in product fabrication. We are moving from age of design heros towards age of maker culture. Time, we spent on reading product reviews is going to be spent more on reading DIY (Do It Yourself) stories. Birth of Fab Labs, maker communities, easy shared instructions and fabrication tools, mean easy access to build your own things to support your personal life individually. This leads into that design education should be aimed for all of us. Goal is to simplify the design process, so it will be fast and easy to adapt by everyone. This workshop is about giving basic tools for polishing your ideas in paper to shine brighter before going to prototype them. The time you spend on paper will pay you back later.

The workshop will be held in English and it will be open for everyone.
